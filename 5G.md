# 5G
---
5G stands for **Fifth Generation** mobile network. 5G network is currently used everywhere around the world. On 5G, it has dramatically improved speed and the massive network capacity has developed new IoT trends, such as smart cities, ehealthcare, IoT in the home or office and connected cars.
